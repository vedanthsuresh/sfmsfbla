FBLA

FBLA, or Future Business Leaders of America, is a student-lead organization that allows hundreds of thousands of students across America to learn about leadership. This business program is the largest one worldwide and greatly helps in training students for real world situations and problems. FBLA allows students from middle school to college to participate in a variety of competitive events along with the opportunity to visit many different cities and entertainment-based events. This club allows students to prosper in terms of business skills such as networking, public speaking, innovation, etc.

Ou r Chapter

SFMS has one of the most competitive FBLA chapters in Georgia. We have won Chapter of the Year six years in a row as of now. Our chapter consistently has participants in NLC (National Leadership Conference) and has won countless amounts of awards and recognitions. SFMS FBLA provides many different opportunities to participate in community service. Some of these opportunities include writing letters for school police officers, clothing drives, giving free school supplies, and more! Our chapter ensures each student has an incredible experience while also enhancing their leadership skills. For example, membership in our chapter can include a fun trip to Six Flags for the FBLA Fall Rally, a trip to Athens, GA for FBLA's Fall Leadership Conference, and a trip to Columbus, GA for FBLA's State Leadership Conference. Doing well in your competitive events can also enable the opportunity to go to San Antonio, Texas, for the National Leadership Conference. Students can also build their leadership skills through guest speakers and officer tryouts. Check this page, your Canvas, and with your parents (if any emails were sent to them) for regular updates and announcements.
